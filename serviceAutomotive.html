Automotive Services

Team Petroleum is a premium wholesale industrial lubricants distributor for car dealers, automotive suppliers, auto repair facilities, and oil change shops, chemical plants and oil refineries in Northern Colorado and the surrounding areas. As one of the top suppliers of industrial lubricants, our company offers only the best products available. We are a proud distributor of quality lubricants from Shell, Chevron, Pennzoil, Quaker State & Shrader Oil.

We also supply other brands to ensure our clients can rest easy knowing that they will receive high-quality products every time they order lubricant from Team Petroleum.

Reliable Delivery Services

Our goal is simple: to quickly and safely deliver top-quality lubricants to our clients. In turn, we help our clients get the best performance out of their machines and vehicles, making their operations more efficient. Similarly, the use of the best lubricants prolongs the lifespan of their equipment and vehicles.

Our delivery area includes Northern Colorado and the surrounding area. We have 140,000 gallons of bulk lubricants storage and over 30,000 square feet of warehouse space to handle all your needs. We also provide free technical support for any questions you may have regarding products in our inventory.
